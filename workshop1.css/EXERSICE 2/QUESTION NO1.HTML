Grilled Cheese Sandwich with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 2 slices of tomato
  • 1 tbsp butter

Instructions

1. Cook the bacon in a pan until crispy, then set aside.
2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
3. Place one slice of bread, buttered side down, in a pan over medium heat.
4. Add a slice of cheese, bacon, tomato, and another slice of cheese on top.
5. Cover with the second slice of bread, buttered side up.
6. Cook until golden brown on one side, then flip and cook the other side.
7. Remove from heat, slice in half, and serve hot.
